### What are Card Chronicles in Keep the Heroes Out!?

Before we dive into the specifics of each ability unlock, let's clarify what card chronicles are in Keep the Heroes Out!. The card chronicles are a series of scenarios that tell a story about the monsters and the dungeons they inhabit. By completing these scenarios in a specific order, players unlock new abilities that can be used in future games.

### A Deep Dive into Ability Unlocks

#### 1. Toasting
The 'Exquisite Cuisine' chronicle unlocks the 'Toasting' ability. This ability allows any allied monster player with at least one meeple in the same room as the active player's meeples to gain the benefits of any potion played. It's an optional ability, so it's up to you whether you want to share your potion powers with allies.

#### 2. Piggyback Riders
Completing the 'Puppy Mom' chronicle grants you the 'Piggyback Riders' ability. This lets a moving monster carry a smaller monster, making it easier to traverse the dungeon. In the Boss Battles expansion, the system has been simplified with monster categories (huge, medium, small) to make it easier to remember who can carry who.

#### 3. Heist Crew
The 'Crime Poetry' chronicle gives you the 'Heist Crew' ability. It allows an active player to discard a card from their hand without using it, allowing a player with a monster meeple in the same room to play one of their cards during the active player's turn. This can be particularly useful in dealing with immediate problems or setting up a powerful attack.

#### 4. Souvenir
Unlock the 'Dungeonpalooza' chronicle to get the 'Souvenir' ability. This allows an active monster player to give another monster player with meeples in the same room a card from their hand. It's a great way to share cards between players and can be especially useful for less crafty clans.

#### 5. Die-Hard
Once you've completed 'Going Corporate', you'll earn the 'Die-Hard' ability. This lets you use epic heroes from scenario 20 in any scenario. These epic heroes add an extra layer of challenge and change how regular heroes behave. Be prepared for a tougher fight, especially in scenarios like 16!

#### 6. Secret Santa
The 'Holiday Special' chronicle offers the 'Secret Santa' ability. This allows each player to draw three cards from the loot deck, gift one to the player on their right, and keep the remaining two. It adds an extra layer of strategy and can provide some unexpected benefits for your monster deck.

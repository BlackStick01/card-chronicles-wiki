## Understanding Card Ranks in Card Chronicles

Card Chronicles ranks are a fundamental aspect of the game's strategy and progression. Each card in the game is assigned a rank, which determines its power, rarity, and the level of expertise required to use it effectively. The ranks are typically divided into tiers, with each tier representing a range of expertise levels.

### Tier Ranks

Tier ranks in Card Chronicles determine how many levels a card can gain and the level areas where such a card can appear. For example, a Tier 5 card can level up to level 6, but it can only be found in areas where Tier 5 cards drop. This creates a sense of progression and encourages players to explore and tackle more challenging areas of the game.

### Expertise Ranks

Expertise ranks, on the other hand, determine which expertise rank you can use a particular card and in which level areas such a card can appear. This means that even if you have a card with a high tier rank, you won't be able to use it until you've reached the required expertise level.
